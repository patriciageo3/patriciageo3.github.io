About

I am a self-thought web-developer who enjoys coding and is enthusiastic about new challenges.

I was first introduced to web-technologies back in 2013, while working for a multinational computer technology corporation. As part of my daily routine, I would manage and create landing pages for corporate events, develop special e-Mail campaigns and update newsletters.

At the end of 2014, I became part of a research agency, where, besides Marketing-related activities, I also got involved in designing and adjusting web-based research instruments, working mostly in HTML/ CSS.

In 2015 and 2016, I worked as a Digital Brand Analyst in the automotive industry, where, in addition to my Marketing reponsibilities, I also developed landing page reports with pertinent results and recommendations to guide the strategies of the Marketing/Media Communication departments.

Lately, I have dedicated my time to deeply understanding and learning web-technologies. Through hard work and dedication, I have sharpened my knowledge of HTML, CSS and JavaScript, while also looking into different frameworks and libraries (jQuery, Bootstrap, NodeJS, and others).
